This relic list appears in several source manuscripts:
(1) It appears in Trinity College Cambridge MS O. 3. 55, f. 2r-v.
(2) It appears in Oxford, Bodleian Library MS Digby 41, on the 'four leaves at the end of the manuscript'.
(3) It appears in Dijon, Biblioth√©que publique, MS 657 (396), f. 39r.
Furthermore, this list is also 'bodily incorporated in the York list'[i.e., LocaSanctorumTextID: Y]. Additionally, an extract of this list was copied into Cambridge University Library Ff. I. 27 on f. 194v of the red chalk foliation [i.e., LocaSanctorumTextID: D3.A*].

According to Battiscombe and Ker, the four leaves at the end of Digby 41 actually belong to a Life of St Cuthbert, British Library, MS Harley 1924. (Battiscombe, Relics of St Cuthbert (1956), 113; Ker, British Museum Quarterly 12(1938): 133.)
